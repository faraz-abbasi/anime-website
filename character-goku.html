Goku

Earth's mightiest defender and a Saiyan raised on Earth.

Character Overview

Goku, born Kakarot, is a Saiyan warrior who crash-landed on Earth and became its greatest protector. Known for his insatiable appetite for training and kindness, he has saved the universe multiple times with his unmatched strength and pure heart.

Notable Traits

  • Super Saiyan transformations
  • User of Kamehameha and Instant Transmission
  • Incredible fighting spirit and love for battle

Series

Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball GT
