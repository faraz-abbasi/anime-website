Monkey D. Luffy

The future Pirate King – Setting sail with dreams, laughter, and a straw hat.

Character Overview

Monkey D. Luffy is the rubber-powered captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the main protagonist of One Piece. With a dream to become the Pirate King, Luffy sails the Grand Line, forming unbreakable bonds with his crew and standing up for freedom and friendship no matter the cost.

Notable Traits

  • User of the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Rubber-Rubber Fruit)
  • Master of all three types of Haki
  • Boundless optimism and unshakable loyalty
