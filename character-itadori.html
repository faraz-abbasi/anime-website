Yuji Itadori

A vessel for Sukuna, driven by compassion and justice.

Character Overview

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. After swallowing a cursed object containing the King of Curses—Ryomen Sukuna—Yuji becomes his vessel. Despite the dangerous power he hosts, Yuji maintains a kind heart and strong resolve to help others and give meaning to the lives of those around him.

Notable Traits

  • Vessel of Ryomen Sukuna
  • Exceptional physical strength and agility
  • User of Divergent Fist and Black Flash

Series

Jujutsu Kaisen and Jujutsu Kaisen 0 (movie)
